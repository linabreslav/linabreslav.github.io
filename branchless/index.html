ABOUT + PROCESS

This project was a user-flow design for a fictional online bank, so-called "Branchless" bank. After conducting a SWOT analysis, I went through a design brainstorming process. I chose to create a whimsical bank that bore little resemblance to one of the major, commonly known banks. I decided to design a fictinal bank that "laughs in the face of big banks" by prioritizing user goals. I chose to create a comical cactus logo and give it a south-western color palette to stand in stark contrast to the traditional colors of typical banks.

After sketching out the flow of the bank, I created wireframes using Sketch. After wireframes, I moved into the mock-up/prototype content phase, and, finally, the prototyping phase in Invision. I created a flow for a user to deposit a check via phone camera, apply the deposit to set precentages, and then review savings goals.


CHALLENGES

My major challenge with the project was taking it from the photoshop stage to the invision stage. Once the screens were inside the prototyping tool, I noticed many changes I wanted to make.
