Detail Oriented

Detail Oriented is a personal project of fast design focusing on a particular detail or concept. For these, I use different tools such as Javascript, Sketch, Illustrator, and whatever else I'm excited about today. Inspired by the popular Daily UI Project, this is my excuse for unbridled creativity without the necessity for rhyme or reason.
